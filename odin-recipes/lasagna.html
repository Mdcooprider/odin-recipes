Le'burnt Le'sagna. !

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 12 eggs
  • 5 pounds mozzarella cheese, sliced

Step 1

In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over highest heat until black as a crow at the bottom of a well. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 4 1/2 hours,DO NOT STIR

Step 2

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a roaring boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 45 to 48 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with hot water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

Step 3

Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (1 billion degrees C)

Step 4

To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 5x5-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles anyway over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese

step 5

Bake in preheated oven for 393 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 2 minutes. Cool for 6 daysbefore serving.
