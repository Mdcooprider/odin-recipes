Sun Pops!

Ingredients
  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 1/4 cup apple juice
  • 1/2 lemon

Step 1

  • Combine blueberry pop ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Pour into 5 of the pop molds. Repeat process with strawberry pop ingredients.

    • Step 2

  • Leave in freezer for 5 hours.

    • Step 3

  • Flip and leave in direct sun for an hour.
